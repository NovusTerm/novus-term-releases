# NovusTerm — Privacy Notice

**Version 1.0 · Effective date: 2026-07-11**

> A Spanish version (*Aviso de Privacidad*) is at [`PRIVACY.es.md`](./PRIVACY.es.md).
> For consumers in Mexico, the Spanish version governs.

NovusTerm is designed to keep your data on your Mac. This notice explains what data
is involved, why, and your choices. It is written to meet the transparency
requirements of Mexico's **Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión
de los Particulares (LFPDPPP, 2025)** and to be useful to users everywhere.

## Who is responsible

The developer of NovusTerm (the **"Developer"**, contact in the EULA §14) is the
data controller (*responsable*) for the limited processing described here. Payments
are handled by **Lemon Squeezy** as Merchant of Record; Lemon Squeezy is responsible
for the personal data involved in the purchase (see its privacy policy at
https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy).

## What data we process, and why

**1. Your app content — stays on your Mac.** Your workspaces, terminal sessions,
settings, SSH profiles, bookmarks, command history, and everything you create are
stored **locally on your device**. We do **not** collect, transmit, host, or have
access to this content. It never reaches our servers — we run none for it.

**2. Purchase data — handled by Lemon Squeezy.** When you buy a subscription, Lemon
Squeezy collects your email and billing details to process the payment, issue an
invoice, and manage your subscription. We receive from Lemon Squeezy only the
information needed to provision and support your license (such as your email and
license/subscription status). We use it to deliver the Software, manage your license,
and provide support. Purpose basis: **necessary** to perform the contract.

**3. License validation.** Your license key is validated against the licensing
provider to confirm your subscription status and device activations. This involves
your license key and a device identifier, not the content of your work.

**4. AI features (opt-in) — sent to your chosen provider, not to us.** If you use the
optional AI features, the content you submit (which may include the active terminal's
context) is sent **directly from your Mac to the third-party AI provider you
configured** (for example Anthropic or OpenAI), using **your own API key**, which is
stored locally in the macOS **Keychain**. **We never receive your API key or the
content you send to the provider.** That provider processes the data under **its own
privacy policy and terms**, which you should review. Purpose basis: **voluntary** —
this only happens if you choose to use the feature.

**5. Automatic updates.** The app checks our public releases feed for updates. This
is a standard network request; we do not use it to build a profile of you.

We do **not** sell your personal data, and we do **not** use it for advertising.

## Sensitive data — your control

Terminal content can contain secrets, credentials, file paths, or personal data. You
decide what, if anything, is sent to an AI provider. We recommend not transmitting
sensitive data you do not need to.

## Your rights (ARCO and others)

Under Mexican law you have the rights of **Acceso, Rectificación, Cancelación, y
Oposición (ARCO)** over your personal data, and the right to limit its use or
disclosure and to revoke consent. Because we hold very little personal data about you
(mainly your email and license status, much of it via Lemon Squeezy), most requests
are simple. If you are in the EU/UK, you have equivalent rights (access,
rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability).

**To exercise any right, or to ask a privacy question, contact us** at the address in
the EULA §14. We respond free of charge (we may recover only reproduction/shipping
costs where the law allows). For purchase data held by Lemon Squeezy, we will help
route your request.

## Retention

We keep license/subscription records for as long as needed to provide the Software
and to meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations (Lemon Squeezy retains the
transaction records as Merchant of Record). Local app data is retained on your device
until you delete it.

## Changes to this notice

We may update this notice; the "Effective date" above reflects the latest version,
and we will make material changes available in-app or on our website. For consumers,
a change never removes a mandatory right.
